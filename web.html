Welcome StreetARTM.D.C.

I am an artist from Craiova, Romania, currently based in the city of Toronto. In my country I am mostly known for "dope painted murals" - breathtaking tridimensional (3D), photorealistic artworks, mostly murals & canvas that radiate/express power, strength but also vulnerability. I combine abstract hues background with realistic emphasized details in order to create masterpiece portraiture pieces. Recently I started developing/transforming my social media artist accounts into digital galleries in order to make my art more visible, approachable and easy to reach by many people as I Intend making Street ART M.D.C. a movement within the Art world, a source of inspiration for others into art, through my passion and dedication. My passion for art was born probably around the same time as me, although not having the proper environment to evolve made me pursue this career later in life. I did finished a bachelor and masters degree in restoration and sacred art but regarding my artwork I am mostly self trained. That is why, one of my special traits is that I can ( and usually do) perform my art with basically any material. From homes to hotels, offices to outdoor murals & land marks, during my career I have been performing my art for different types of customers. I also had collaborations with big brands like : Moncler, Daf, Kaufland, Julius Meinl and more. However, my biggest passion is participating in art festivals as I usually perform my art as a statement and I want it to be accessible to people during process in order to bring people closer to art. One of my most known festival pieces is "Seeing the future" done at ZiDArt festival with airlight purifying air paint.
